# Product Strategy

## North Star

Build the most trusted open intelligence pipeline for rapid ingestion, safe normalization, risk scoring, and analyst-ready search.

## 2026 Objectives

1. Improve mean time to insight under 3 minutes from ingestion.
2. Reach 99.9% platform availability for core processing.
3. Enforce zero critical CVEs in production images.
4. Cut false-positive risk labels by 30% through model iteration.

## Differentiators

1. Privacy-preserving collection options.
2. Multi-language enrichment roadmap.
3. Explainable risk labels and scoring provenance.
4. Analyst workflow-first dashboards and saved queries.
